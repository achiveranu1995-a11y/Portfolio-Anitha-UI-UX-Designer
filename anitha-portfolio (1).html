🏠 Chore Buddy
Mobile App · UI/UX Case Study · 2024
📋 Problem

Household chores often lead to conflict, miscommunication, and unequal workload distribution among families and roommates. There was no engaging way to assign, track, and celebrate task completion together.

💡 Solution

Chore Buddy is a collaborative task management app that makes household management fun and fair through gamification, smart scheduling, and real-time progress tracking.

✨ Key Features
Shared Task Board Point & Reward System Recurring Schedules Push Notifications Household Groups Progress Dashboard Dark Mode Support Accessibility First
🔍 Design Process

Started with user interviews and surveys to identify pain points. Created user personas and journey maps, followed by low-fidelity wireframes in Miro. Progressed to high-fidelity prototypes in Figma with multiple usability testing rounds.

🏆 Outcome
Users reported a 70% improvement in task clarity and fairness perception. The gamified approach increased engagement and reduced arguments about chores — turning a daily friction point into a shared positive habit.
🍳 Let's Cook
Mobile App · UI/UX Case Study · 2024
📋 Problem

Home cooks struggled to find recipes matching their pantry ingredients, dietary restrictions, and skill level. Existing apps were overwhelming, cluttered, and rarely considered accessibility needs.

💡 Solution

Let's Cook is an intelligent recipe discovery app with smart pantry tracking, step-by-step cooking guidance, and strong accessibility features — designed for everyone from beginners to seasoned cooks.

✨ Key Features
Smart Pantry Tracking Dietary Filters Step-by-step Mode Timer Integration Voice Assistance Save & Collections Large Text Support High Contrast Mode
🔍 Design Process

Conducted competitive analysis of 8 cooking apps. Created affinity diagrams from user research insights. Designed with a strong accessibility-first approach — tested with users who have visual impairments and mobility challenges. Iterated three prototype versions based on usability feedback.

🏆 Outcome
Achieved excellent usability scores in testing, with users completing cooking tasks 40% faster than on competitor apps. Accessibility features were highlighted as a key differentiator, making the app usable for a much broader audience.
